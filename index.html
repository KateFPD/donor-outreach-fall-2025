Fall 2025 Donor Outreach

Primary communications page for letters, objectives, and contact links.

Primary Goal

Inspire lasting partnerships with new and returning donors for Fall 2025 by sharing impactful stories, highlighting the difference every contribution makes, and inviting donors to be part of a mission that transforms lives and communities.

Objectives

  • Engage with Impact – Craft compelling donor outreach letters (corporate, individual, and community) that clearly show how contributions create real change.
  • Expand Connections – Build and segment contact lists across email, LinkedIn, social, and print to reach and welcome new supporters.
  • Drive Campaign Momentum – Coordinate a clear timeline of outreach efforts to maximize visibility and donor engagement.
  • Strengthen Trust – Track responses, pledges, and contributions transparently, ensuring donors see the direct results of their support.
  • Foster Collaboration – Provide consistent updates to the director, team, and donors, reinforcing trust and shared progress.
