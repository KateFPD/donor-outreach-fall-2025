Donor Outreach & Requests – Fall 2025

Welcome to our project page. Below you can find our goals, objectives, and important links for donors and partners.

🌟 Primary Goal

To build genuine connections with our donors and invite them to be part of the impact we’re creating for Fall 2025. This project is about reaching out thoughtfully — sharing our story, listening to theirs, and making sure they understand how their support directly helps our mission.

✅ Objectives

  • Prepare and send donor outreach letters (corporate, individual, community).
  • Build and segment contact lists (email, LinkedIn, social, print).
  • Coordinate campaign timeline and outreach dates.
  • Track responses, pledges, and contributions in Planner.
  • Provide weekly updates to the director and team.
